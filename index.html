Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

Our school hours are from 6:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. However, specific class schedules vary by age group.

What is the admission process?

The admission process involves submitting an application form, attending an interview, and completing the enrollment process upon acceptance.

Do you provide meals for students?

Yes, we provide nutritious meals for all students. Our menu is designed to meet the dietary needs of growing children.

What is your teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain a low teacher-to-student ratio to ensure personalized attention. For preschool, it's 1:10, and for higher grades, it's 1:15.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we offer transportation services for students living within a certain radius of the school. Additional fees apply.
