Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us and visiting our school.

What is the best time to visit the school?

The best time to visit is during school hours (8:00 AM - 3:00 PM) on weekdays when you can observe classes in session. We recommend scheduling visits between 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM or 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM to avoid disrupting lunch and break times.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to email inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Phone calls are answered immediately during office hours (6:00 AM - 5:00 PM). For urgent matters, WhatsApp messages are usually responded to within a few hours.

Can I visit without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure our admissions team is available to give you personalized attention and a comprehensive tour of our facilities.

What should I bring during my visit?

Please bring a list of questions you'd like to ask, any previous school records if transferring, and your child if possible. We also recommend bringing a notebook to jot down important information during the tour.

Do you offer virtual tours or online consultations?

Yes! We offer virtual school tours via video call for parents who cannot visit in person. We can also conduct online consultations to discuss your child's educational needs and our programs. Contact us to schedule a virtual meeting.
