Join Our School Family

We welcome families who share our vision for quality education and holistic development.

At Perfect Foundation Academy, we believe that every child deserves access to quality education in a nurturing environment. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and supportive, ensuring that both parents and children feel comfortable and confident in their decision to join our school family.

We accept applications throughout the year, subject to availability of places. However, we encourage early applications to secure a spot for your child.

Our Admissions Process

A simple step-by-step guide to enrolling your child at Perfect Foundation Academy.

1

Inquiry

Contact our admissions office via phone, email, or in person to express your interest and gather information about our programs.

2

School Tour

Schedule a visit to our campus to tour the facilities, meet our staff, and get a feel for our learning environment.

3

Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form along with the required documents and application fee.

4

Assessment/Interview

Depending on the age and grade level, your child may be invited for an assessment or interview to determine their educational needs.

5

Admission Decision

You will receive an admission decision within two weeks of completing the assessment/interview process.

6

Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete the enrollment process by paying the required fees and submitting any additional documents.

Required Documents

Please prepare the following documents for the application process.

For All Applicants

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of child's birth certificate
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (4)
  • Immunization records
  • Medical information form

For Transfer Students (Additional)

  • Academic records from previous school
  • Transfer certificate
  • Recommendation letter (if available)
  • Most recent report card

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our admissions process.

What is the application deadline?

We accept applications throughout the year, subject to availability. However, for the main academic year starting in September, we recommend applying by May to secure a place.

Is there an application fee?

Yes, there is a non-refundable application fee of GHS 100. This fee covers the administrative costs of processing your application.

What is the assessment process like?

The assessment process varies by age group. For younger children (Toddler to KG), it involves an informal observation session. For older students (Basic 1-JHS 3), there will be academic assessments in core subjects to determine their current level.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

We offer a limited number of merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid. Please contact our admissions office for more information about eligibility criteria and application process.

Can my child join mid-year?

Yes, we accept mid-year admissions subject to availability of places. Each case is considered individually to ensure a smooth transition for the child.

What is your policy on age requirements?

Children must meet the minimum age requirement for their respective class by September 1st of the academic year. For example, children entering Toddler class must be 2 years old by September 1st.
